# Privacy Policy for ClockIn

**Effective Date:** February 15, 2026

Thank you for using ClockIn.

ClockIn respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

---

## 1. Data Collection and Storage

ClockIn stores user-entered work time information locally on your device using internal storage.

ClockIn:

- Does NOT collect personal data
- Does NOT transmit any data to external servers
- Does NOT share data with third parties
- Does NOT use analytics services
- Does NOT use advertising services
- Does NOT access your contacts, location, camera, or other sensitive device data

All data remains stored exclusively on your device.

---

## 2. Internet Usage

ClockIn works fully offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

---

## 3. Data Security

Because all information is stored locally on your device, you maintain full control over your data. The app does not have the ability to access or retrieve your information remotely.

---

## 4. Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by:

- Clearing the app data from your device settings, or
- Uninstalling the ClockIn app from your device

Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data.

## 5. Children's Privacy

ClockIn does not knowingly collect, store, or share personal information from children or any users.
